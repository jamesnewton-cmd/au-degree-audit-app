# Accounting Major Requirements (2022-23, 68 credit hours)

## Business Core (43 hours)
- MATH 1300 Finite Mathematics OR MATH 1400 Applied Calculus OR MATH 2010 Calculus I
- ACCT 2010 Principles of Accounting I (3)
- ACCT 2020 Principles of Accounting II (3)
- BSNS 1050 Business as a Profession (2)
- BSNS 2310 Business Analytics (3)
- BSNS 2450 Data Analytics and Decision Making for Business (3)
- BSNS 2510 Principles of Finance (3)
- BSNS 2710 Principles of Management (3)
- BSNS 2810 Principles of Marketing (3)
- BSNS 3270 Project Management (3)
- BSNS 3420 Business Law (3)
- BSNS 4500 Strategic Management (3)
- BSNS 4910 Senior Seminar in Business (2)
- ECON 2010 Principles of Macroeconomics (3)
- ECON 2020 Principles of Microeconomics (3)

## Accounting Requirements (25 hours)
- ACCT 3010 Intermediate Accounting I (3)
- ACCT 3020 Intermediate Accounting II (3)
- ACCT 3110 Managerial Accounting (3)
- ACCT 3500 Accounting Information Systems (3)
- ACCT 4020 Federal Income Tax (3)
- ACCT 4050 Advanced Federal Income Tax (3)
- ACCT 4100 Advanced Financial Accounting (3)
- ACCT 4310 Auditing (3)
- ACCT 4900 Professional Ethics for Accountants (1)

## Notes
- ACCT 4900 is a Writing Intensive course in the LA Program
- BSNS 4910 is a Writing Intensive course in the LA Program
- ACCT 4850 Practicum in CPA Exam Preparation (1-4 hrs) - highly recommended, NOT required
